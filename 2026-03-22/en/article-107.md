# Article 107

Magistrates are irremovable. They may not be dismissed or suspended from service nor transferred to other seats or functions except following a decision by the High Council of the Judiciary, adopted either for the reasons and with the guarantees of defense established by the judicial organization or with their consent.

The Minister of Justice has the power to initiate disciplinary action.

Magistrates differ from each other only in their functions.

The Public Prosecutor enjoys the guarantees established in their regard by the rules on judicial organization.
