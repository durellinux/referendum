# Article 110

Without prejudice to the powers of the High Council of the Judiciary, the organization and functioning of services relating to justice belong to the Minister of Justice [cf. [Art. 107 para. 2](https://www.senato.it/istituzione/la-costituzione/parte-ii/titolo-iv/sezione-i/articolo-107)].
