# Article 106

Appointments of magistrates take place through competitive examination.

The law on judicial organization [cf. [Art. 108](https://www.senato.it/istituzione/la-costituzione/parte-ii/titolo-iv/sezione-ii/articolo-108)] may allow the appointment, even elective, of honorary magistrates for all functions assigned to single judges.

Upon designation by the High Council of the Judiciary, full professors of law at universities and lawyers with fifteen years of practice and registered in the special rolls for higher jurisdictions may be called to the office of counselors of the Court of Cassation for outstanding merit.

Source: [Senato della Repubblica - Article 106](https://www.senato.it/istituzione/la-costituzione/parte-ii/titolo-iv/sezione-i/articolo-106)
