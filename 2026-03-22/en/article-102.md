# Article 102

Judicial functions are exercised by ordinary magistrates established and regulated by the rules on the judicial organization, which also regulate the distinct careers of judges and prosecutors [cf. [Art. 108](https://www.senato.it/istituzione/la-costituzione/parte-ii/titolo-iv/sezione-ii/articolo-108)].

Extraordinary judges or special judges may not be established [cf. [Art. 25 para. 1](https://www.senato.it/istituzione/la-costituzione/parte-i/titolo-i/articolo-25)]. Only specialized sections for certain matters may be established within ordinary judicial bodies, also with the participation of eligible citizens from outside the judiciary [cf. [VI](https://www.senato.it/istituzione/la-costituzione/disposizioni-transitorie-e-finali)].

The law regulates the cases and forms of the direct participation of the people in the administration of justice.

Source: [Senato della Repubblica - Article 102](https://www.senato.it/istituzione/la-costituzione/parte-ii/titolo-iv/sezione-i/articolo-102)
